Thick Cut French Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 stick of butter
  • 3 Whole large eggs
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla
  • 1/4 cup of cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • Whole loaf of thick bread like brioche or challah

Cooking Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and set a gridded rack or tray off to the side of where you will be cooking with your pan.
  2. Slice roughly 4 1/2 inch thick slices of the bread.
  3. In a large bowl, thoroughly whisk together the eggs and heavy cream. Add the vanilla and cinnamon.
  4. Heat about 4 tbsp of butter in a non-stick or properly seasoned carbon steel pan. Add more butter after each slice or set of sclices is cooked.
  5. Thoroughly soak the bread in the custard and lay down away from you on the pan. Depending on the size of the pan, one to three slices may fit comfortably.
  6. Cook until golden brown and place the slice/slices on the rack that was set aside. This rack can be tossed in to the oven to keep the french toast warm and even cook it a little more as you cook the remaining slices.
