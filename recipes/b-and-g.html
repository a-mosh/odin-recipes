Hearty Biscuits and Gravy

A dish perfect for cold days and a nap

Ingredients

  • Refridgerated canned biscuits
  • 1lb pork sausage or your favorite ground breakfast sausage
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Green onion
  • Salt & pepper

Cooking Procedure

  1. Cut small diagonal sections of green onion for later as a garnish.
  2. Cook the biscuits according to the packaging.
  3. Cook sausage in a pan until at least 165F.
  4. Add about 1/2 cup of flour
  5. Add the milk and stir until a gravy begins to form. Adjust flour and milk until the gravy is a desired consitency.
  6. Bring gravy to a boil and reduce to a simmer for 5 minutes. Season with salt & pepper to taste.
  7. Place biscuits on a plate and add the desired amount of gravy.
  8. Finish by garnishing with the green onions.
