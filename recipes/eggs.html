The Perfect Scrambled Eggs

Easy to make scrambled eggs with a dash of chives

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 3 whole large eggs
  • 1 Tbsp chives
  • Salt & pepper

Cooking Procedure

  1. Finely chop the chives to be used as a garnish at the end.
  2. Heat a non-stick or a properly seasoned carbon steel pan over medium heat.
  3. Place the 2Tbsp of butter in to the pan until it is mostly melted.
  4. Crack the 3 whole eggs into the pan and stir until the eggs are scrambled.
  5. Continue to fold and stir the eggs until cooked to a nice, fluffy texture.
  6. Season to taste with salt & pepper and garnish with the chopped chives.
